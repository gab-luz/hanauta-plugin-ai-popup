The capital city of Russia is Moscow.

🇷🇺 More about Moscow

  • It's not just the capital, but also the largest city in Russia and the most populous metropolitan area in Europe.
  • Moscow sits on the Moskva River in Central Russia.
  • It's a huge hub for politics, economics, culture, and science.
  • While Moscow has been the capital for much of history, the capital moved to Saint Petersburg in the 18th century before returning in 1918.
